Creating European Orders reports

Get information about where your sales are coming from and the dollar amount of total sales on an international basis. A pie chart displays your European sales efforts where your products are currently selling, including the order count and order amount.

To create a European Orders report

  1. In Analytics, select Order Reports > European Orders.

  3. Choose to view by Details or Orders and click Search.

  4. Click Export to Excel for a printout of your total European orders and sales figures. The status description remains on screen even if you export the results to Excel.
