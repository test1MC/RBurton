Creating Inventory On Hand reports

The Inventory on Hand Report shows your inventory count. Use this report to audit your physical inventory and make adjustments for discrepancies. The report contains a list of all non-deleted SKUs available in your site's database. You can sort the list by:

  • SKU,
  • Name,
  • Stock Status,
  • Active Status,
  • Inventory Count,
  • Cost, Selling Price,
  • On Sale Price,
  • Current Inventory Value.

To create an Inventory On Hand report

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > Inventory on Hand.
  2. To sort alphanumerically on specific criteria, click a column heading, for example, Stock Status. Click the same heading again to sort in the other direction (for example, highest to lowest).
