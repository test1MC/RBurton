Creating Top Customers reports

Use the Top Customers Report to identify and keep track of your top customers, listed in order of the dollar amount spent on your site. Report data is displayed in a bar chart and data table showing top customer information, so you can quickly see who your best customers are.

To create a Top Customer report

  1. In Analytics, select Customer Reports > Top Customers.

  4. Select the Order Status of orders to include in the report.

  5. Click the Search button.
  6. To send an email to a customer listed in the report, click on that customer's email address.
