Creating US States reports

The US States report shows sales information broken down by state. Results are displayed in a pie chart and table, showing sales efforts from all states in which your products are currently selling, including number of items per state and the dollar amounts of those sales.

To create a US States report

  1. In Analytics, select Order Reports > US States.
  3. Click the clock icons to choose a time frame for the report.
  5. Use the Order Status list to filter by completed, or shipped and completed orders, or select to display all.
  6. To display only orders from Canadian customers, select the Include Canadian Provinces check box.
  7. Click the Search button .
  8. Click any View Details button to see more DMAdirect market area data for that state.
