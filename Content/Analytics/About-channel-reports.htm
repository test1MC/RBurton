About channel reports

You can monitor which of your affiliates is increasing traffic to your site, bringing in orders, and/or driving revenue. You can stay up to date on your Amazon settlement report straight from the Aptos Site Manager. You have the ability to view and manage data returned by fulfillment centers to keep incoming and outgoing invoices easily managed to the specific order to which the invoice is associated. You can stay up to date on the success of your marketing efforts and membership programs and check on the status and success of your promotional campaigns. Through Aptos Channel Reports, you can stay up-to-date on the progress of your SMS campaigns, Promo codes, affiliate sales, and more.

The Channel Reports area includes:

  • Affiliate Sales
  • Amazon
  • Fulfillment Settlement
  • Historical Affiliate Tracking
  • Promo Code (Detailed)
  • Promo Code (Summary)
