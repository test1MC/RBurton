Creating Amazon reports

The Amazon report provides you with revenue information via the channel, and tells you which orders are settled and unsettled, what status your outstanding orders are in, and it gives you an actionable view of unpaid items.

If you notice any discrepancies in this report, remember that Amazon orders must be marked as shipped in order to pay a client.

To create an Amazon report

  1. In Analytics, select Channel Reports > Amazon.
  2. Use the lists to select a Date From and Date Thru range.

  3. Click Search.

    Three 3D pie charts and tables display your settlement details By Dollars, By Count, and Unsettled Orders by Status. Two data tables with information on Paid Items and Unpaid Items also appear at the bottom of the screen, .

  4. Click Export to Excel for a printable version of the Paid and Unpaid Items report.
