Creating Scheduled reports

Clients can request basic reports to be emailed to them at specific times of the day. Once this report is selected, you can select the time for the report to run, a list of recipients, and create a custom email subject.

You can schedule three kinds of reports:

  • Inventory Below Threshold - Informs the recipient when items marked as such fall below inventory thresholds configured in the system.
  • Quick Daily Transactions - Emails the order totals on a daily basis for the current day and the previous 10 days.
  • Subscription Stock - Alerts the recipient when available inventory for subscription items fall below a pre-configured threshold.

To Schedule a report

  1. In Analytics, choose User Reports > Scheduled Reports.
  2. Use the check boxes to Enable the reports you want..

  3. For each report you've enabled:

    • add Subject text for the email subject line.
    • In the Email to box, enter recipient email addresses, one per line

    • Use the Scheduled For list to schedule the time you want the report to be sent.

      Note: Site Manager is on CSTcentral standard time, so you may have to convert the time you select to compensate for this.

  4. Click the Save button, and emails containing the reports will be sent to the specified addresses.
