Creating Profit Margin reports

If you enter cost at the product level, you can use the Profit Margin Report to measure the profit from your sales over a specified period of time. Use the Profit Margin Report to measure profit, not just base revenue, from your sales over a specified period of time. This report allows you to break the profit margin down to affiliate campaigns.

To create a Profit Margin report to measure profit over a specified time period

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > Profit Margin.
  3.  Click the Affiliate list to choose either all affiliates, or a specific individual affiliate or customer.
  4. From the Order Filter list, choose to create your report based on:
    1. All Orders
    2. Only Completed Orders
    3. Shipped and Completed Orders.
  5. Select the payment type from the Payment Type drop-down.
  6. Click Search.
  7. To export the report to Excel, click the Export to Excel button at the bottom of the screen.

Details for report columns are:

Column Details
Total Cost The cost stored in the system at the time of the order * total units for the specified line item.
Total Gross The selling price this customer paid for the specified line item * total units purchased of this line item.
Item Coupon Total The prorated amount of the promotion code value against the line item, since we store promo amounts at the order level.
Order Coupon Total The total order level coupon amount. (If there was a discount/promo during that time period, this area calculates the total promotional discount within the date range.)
Tax Item level tax
Shipping Item level shipping
Total Price Total of item * qty + shipping/tax for this line item.
Total Net Total Gross minus Total Cost
Total Margin The percentage of cost compared with gross.
