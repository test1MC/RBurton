Creating No Stock reports

The No Stock report provides information on out of stock inventory, listed by both supplier and reason. By default, EOM generates a the report based on the last 30 days; you can select the time frame you need.

To create a No Stock report

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > No Stock.
  2. To select a dates other than the default, click the lists to select a Date From and Date Thru range.
  3. Click the Search.
  4. To export either the no stock items BY SUPPLIER or BY REASON area of report to an Excel spreadsheet for reporting or printing, click its corresponding Export to Excel button.
