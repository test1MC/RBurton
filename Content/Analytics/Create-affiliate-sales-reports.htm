Creating Affiliate Sales reports

Use this report to manage affiliate sales. Check to see which of your affiliatesor marketing channels you’ve created as affiliates for tracking purposes is increasing traffic to your site, bringing in orders, and/or driving revenue. View the CPC and CPA totals for your affiliates here as well. In this report, you can also view results from natural search and untracked sales.

The report automatically defaults to the past 30 days, and displays the Order Status of “All”.

Two pie charts display the number of orders by affiliate, Top Affiliate Orders, and the revenue by affiliate, Top Affiliate Dollars.

To create an Affiliate Sales report

  1. In Analytics, select Channel Reports > Affiliate Sales.
  2. Use the lists to select a Date From and Date Thru range for which you want to view affiliate sales.
  3. Click the Affiliate list and choose either all affiliates, or a specific individual affiliate or customer.
  4. Click the Order Status llist and select types of orders you would like included in the report.

  5. Click the Search button.

  6.  Click on a pie chart segment to slide it out of the chart, to make it easier to see. Click again to slide the segment back into place.

About report data

In addition to the pie charts , the report also provides tabular data at the bottom of the page. The data includes:

  • Affiliate name
  • Orders
  • Total Revenue
  • CPA (percentage paid to Affiliate)
  • Total CPA (total dollar amount paid to Affiliate)
  • Visitors
  • CPC (cost per click – individual)
  • Total CPC (cost per click – total).

This information is displayed by affiliate and is totaled at the bottom for the selected date range.
