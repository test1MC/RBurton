Creating Detailed reports

Use the detailed product report to see a detailed breakdown of products sold in a given month. From this report, you can see the number of orders on a specific day, or the revenue that it generated.

To created a Detailed product report

  1. From Analytics, select Product Reports > Detailed.

  2. Use lists and fields above the report to filter search results by Month, Year, Type, Sub-Category, Channel, and Payment Method, and click the Search button.
