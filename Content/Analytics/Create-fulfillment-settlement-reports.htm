Creating Fulfillment Settlement reports

Use this report to view and manage data returned by fulfillment centers, and to keep incoming and outgoing invoices easily managed to the specific order to which the invoice is associated.

To create a Fulfillment Settlement report

  1. In Analytics, select Channel Reports > Fulfillment Settlement.
  3. Click the Date Method list, and choose whether you want to run the report based on Invoice Date or Order Date.

  4. Click Search.

    This report does not include the individual items of each order.

    The report shows Settled Orders and/or Unsettled Orders.

Navigating in the report

You can navigate through three levels of reporting by triggering a search based on the dates. The first and default level is the Order level. From here, it expands out to the invoice level and then to the invoice item.
