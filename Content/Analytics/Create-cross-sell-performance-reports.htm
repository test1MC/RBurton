Create Cross-sell Performance reports

This report shows which products consumers bought with other products, and can help you to build or improve post-checkout workflow.

You can select from two different sets of cross-sell information between a specified date range.

  • By Revenue results show how much revenue was generated when products were bought together (in the same cart).
  • By Views results show how many times a product was viewed by a customer when considering another product.

Three sets of information are displayed:

  • a pie chart indicating either the number of times a product was bought with another, or the number of times a product was viewed with another.
  • a color-coded legend to the pie chart indicating which two products are being paired.
  • a table providing additional details about the cross-sell conditions.

Use this information to build out a post-checkout workflow based on consumer behavior, or tweak an existing one to improve conversion during post-checkout.

To create a Cross-sell Performance report

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > Cross-Sell Performance.
  2. Select a date range and a report type.
  3. Click the Search button.
