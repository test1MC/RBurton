Creating Top Page Views reports

Use this report to examine the top 20 pages most visited on your site, including views by page type, category pages, sub-category pages, brand pages and product pages.

The report's five pie charts and their corresponding data tables on the page are:

  • Top 20 Page Views by Page Type
  • Top 20 Category Pages
  • Top 20 Sub-Category Pages
  • Top 20 Brand Pages
  • Top 20 Product Pages

The pie charts display the percentage breakdown of top pages based on visits recorded for each. Data tables displayed next to each pie chart list the top pages along with the corresponding number of total visits for each page over the specified date range.

To create a Top Page Views report

  1. In Analytics, select Traffic > Top Page Views.
  3. Click the Search button.
  4. Use charts' special features:

    1. Hover over any section of a pie chart to display its page name.

    2. Click on any section of a pie chart to separate it slightly from the rest of the chart, making it easier to see.
    3. Click on any of the page icons in the data table to identify that page in the pie chart.
