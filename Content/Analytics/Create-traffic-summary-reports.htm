Creating Traffic Summary reports

The Traffic Summary report provides high level data concerning traffic to your site. You can also use this report to view traffic data by Browser, Search Engine Spider, Mobile Browser, Other Sources, or Search Keywords. Report data is shown in seven charts:

Summary chart

Traffic by browser

Traffic by search engine spider

Traffic by mobile browser

Other traffic

Search keywords

Traffic (last 30 days)

Use this data to analyze where traffic originated from before coming to your site. Compare source, number of visitors, number of orders, order amount, and paid commissions. Note that:

  • The Order Amount is the total of all items in the order, less shipping and taxes.
  • The Order Total includes the Order Amount plus shipping and taxes (less discount).

Note: In some cases the Order Total shown may be less than the Order Amount because of discounts.

To create a Traffic Summary report

  1. In Analytics, select Traffic Reports > Summary.
  3. Click the Search button.
