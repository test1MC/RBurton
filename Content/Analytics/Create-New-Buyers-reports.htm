Creating New Buyers reports

The New Buyers report searches for buyer activity within the time period you specify. The report shows only those buyers that have not purchased before, and are not declined or have canceled purchases.

The report can be filtered by state, and by Tier Group. This is reflected at the order level, the affiliate and customer tiers that were assigned to the customer at the time of the order.

To create a New Buyers report

  1. In Analytics, select Customer Reports > New Buyers.
  3. Use the clock icons to select from and thru times of day to refine your search if needed.
  5. Use the State list to select a state on which to filter, or choose All Orders.
  6. Click an order number in the report to display the order history screen, and manage the order as needed.
