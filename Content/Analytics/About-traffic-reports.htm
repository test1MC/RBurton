About traffic reports

Traffic can help you track origins of traffic to your site, how your customers heard about you, referring URLs, site statistics, trends, and wishlists.

Traffic reports can show you by location where your orders are coming from, whether in the US or worldwide. You can also gauge your conversion success rates through our comprehensive cart conversion report. Find out how your customers heard about you and get an at-a-glance view of your search engine rankings for your desired search terms. The source report is a great way to see the search terms that your visitors are using to find your products. You can also get a high level view of affiliate performance, search engine spidering, and what browsers are being used to view your site. Examine the pages most visited on your site, obtain views of the highs and lows of the traffic driven to your storefront, and manage your customer wish lists with the click of a button.

The Traffic Reports area contains the sections:

  • 404/Detailed Traffic
  • By Location
  • Cart Conversion
  • Heard From
  • Summary
  • Search Terms
  • Top Page Views
  • Trends
  • Wish Lists
