Creating Repeat Purchase Detailed reports

Use this report to see a breakdown of the repeat orders during that time period in a detailed view, and identify who your best repeat customers are. You can create the report for all 50 states, or for specific ones. You can also use the information gathered from this report to create an email campaign.

To create a Repeat Purchase Detailed report

  1. In Analytics, select Customer Reports > Repeat Purchase Detailed.
  4. Select the State to include in the report, or choose All States.
  5. Select the Order Status of orders to include in the report.
  6. Select whether to include Order Numbers in the report.
  7. To add a customer to the email list of repeat customers, click their email address.

  8. After adding customers to the email list, you can send an email to this list of repeat customers. To do this, scroll to the bottom of the page and click the Email button.

    Note: Addresses are not displayed until the data table is populated—customer information is saved in the same way as it is from the Customized Order Report page. Clicking the Email button takes you to the Marketing > Email > Create pagr, where you can create and schedule a newsletter email to send to these customers.
