Creating Inventory Sell Through reports

Sell through provides a composite measure of sales and inventory, and helps evaluate item performance. You typically evaluate sell through daily for fast moving products or weekly for slower moving or replenishment based products. A higher value is better, indicating your sales velocity is good and your inventory is appropriately forecast. Low sell through indicates either poor sales, or too much inventory. In most cases, sell through for an item is compared in recent periods like current week and last week, as well as in aggregate for several months or even a year.

When evaluating sell through, you can use this reporting tool to sort by a period of time, a brand, a sub-category, or even a specific product or product option.

To create an Inventory Sell Through report

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > Inventory Sell Through.
  2. Use lists to select date ranges, and Brand and Sub-Category as needed.
  3. Select whether to Show All Products.
  4. Optionally, also provide Product or Option SKU/Name text on which to filter in the Text Filter field.
  5. Click the Search button.
  6. To sort alphanumerically on specific criteria, click a column heading, for example, Brand. Click the same heading again to sort in the other direction (for example, highest to lowest).
  7. You may need to scroll to the bottom of the page to see this button.
