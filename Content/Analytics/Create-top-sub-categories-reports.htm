Creating Top Sub-categories reports

Use this report to view the sub-categories driving the most traffic on your site. This report provides an overview of the traffic, revenue, and the number of units sold by sub-category within a chosen period of time. This report provides an overview of three sub-categories, each in its own section of the report:

  • Traffic by Sub-Category, shows the total number of views by sub-category in the filtered date range.
  • Revenue by Sub-Category, shows the total revenue by sub-category in the filtered date range.
  • Units Sold by Sub-Category, shows the total units sold by sub-category in the filtered date range.

To create a Top Sub-category report

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > Top Sub-Categories.
  2. To filter by date, select a From Date and Thru Date range from the lists, and click the Search button. Scroll down to see all three sections of the report.
  3. Hover your mouse cursor over any section in a pie chart to view the percentages.
  4. Click on a section to separate it from the pie chart to make it easier to see. Click on it a second time to return it to its original position in the chart.
