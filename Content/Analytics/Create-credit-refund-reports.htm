Creating Credit/Refund reports

Use this report to view a breakdown of credits and refunds including the reason for the credit/refund, total credit amount per date, and total credit amount per state over the specified date range.

The report includes a pie chart and three data tables:

  • a pie chart and associated first data table breaks down the credits/refunds based on reason (for example, exchange, could not ship, buyer canceled, and so on).
  • the middle data table displays credit totals and credit amounts based on specific dates.
  • the bottom data table shows credit totals and amounts broken down by US States.

To create a Credit/Refund report

  1. In Analytics, select User Reports > Credit/Refund Report.
  3. Click Search.
  4. To export data in any of the three tables of data for reporting or printing, click its Export to Excel button .
