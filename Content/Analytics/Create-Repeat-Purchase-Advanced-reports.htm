Creating Repeat Purchase Advanced reports

Use the Advanced Repeat Purchase report to learn about the behavior of repeat purchase customers. You can use this information to market your products more effectively. The report allows for both a summary and detailed view, and several filters to find data by demographics.

The report consists of a data table and the summary bar at the top of the report showing how many distinct customers ordered within the date range totaling the number of orders as well as their value.

To create a Repeat Purchase Advanced report

  1. In Analytics, select Customers > Repeat Purchase Advanced.
  2. In the Starting Date Range area and Re-Ordered Date Range area, use the lists to select a date range and times.
    1. Starting Date Range identifies those customers who made a purchase within that time frame.
    2. Re-Ordered Date Range identifies customers found in the Starting Date Range who re-ordered.
  3. In the Reporting Options area:
    1. Select the State to include in the report, or choose All States.
    2. Select the Order Status.

    4. Use the Qty: list and field to filter on the number of times a customer has purchased,

      For example: Qty: > 3 displays only customers who made more than three purchases during the date range specified.
    5. Select whether to show a Detailed ReportIf you select Detailed Report, the report displays detailed data including email, name, city, state, and basic order data..

  4. Note: You may need to scroll to the bottom of the report to see this button.

  5. To send an email newsletter containing promotional or other information to those customers, click the eMail button.
