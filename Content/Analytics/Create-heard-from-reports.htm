Creating Heard From reports

This report helps you to understand how your customers are finding your website.

Note: Aptos recommends making the Heard From section of your check-out process optional. If you make Heard Frommandatory, many customers will check off the first option they see in order to speed through checkout, and this can lead to inaccurate metrics.

Data is displayed in a 3D pie chart showing a percentage breakdown of your customers “heard from” selections. A data table beside the chart shows the “heard from” medium (magazine, newsletter, and so onYou can change these settings in CRM > Communications > Listings.), the corresponding number of people who came from each source, and the total dollar amount spent by those customers.

To create a Heard From report

  1. In Analytics, select Traffic Reports > Heard From.
  3. Select whether to Include Declined Orders in the report.
  4. Click Search.
