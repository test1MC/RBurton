Creating Shipping Cost reports

Use this report to evaluate shipping costs and adjust business decisions accordingly. This report is especially useful if you use multiple suppliers or drop-shippers.

Note: The Shipping Cost report appears only if you use QuickShip with .

To create a Shipping Cost report

  1. In Analytics, select Product Reports > Shipping Cost.
  3. From the Supplier list, choose either all suppliers, or a specific one.
  4. Click Search.
