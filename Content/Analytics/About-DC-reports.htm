About Digital Commerce reports

With Digital Commerce you can view search engine reports, traffic reports, order reports, and more. These reports offer a true understanding of your business.

Report Description
Order reports The Aptos platform offers you over 60 customized order reports. You can use this tool to gain insight into metrics around traffic, sales, marketing initiatives, and more. You can run reports on items such as daily trends, payments, repeat purchases, sales trends and top customers.
Product reports You can customize product reports. For example, you can create an inventory velocity report which provides information on the recent sales of each SKU as well as an estimate of how long the existing quantity will last. Use the most viewed report to analyze your most viewed products. Use the productivity report to view a list of your most popular items and see a breakdown of order totals and shipping for the items. You can pull reports on products by sub-category and use the profit margin report to measure the profit, and not just base revenue, for your sales over a time period. Analyze your shipping costs, view top categories and more.
Traffic reports Traffic reports show you by location where your orders are coming from, whether in the US or worldwide. You can gauge your conversion success rates through our comprehensive cart conversion report. Find out how your customers heard about you and get an at-a-glance view of your search engine rankings. The source report is a great way to see the search terms that your visitors are using to find your products. You can also see affiliate performance, search engine spidering, and what browsers are used to view your site.
Channel reports Monitor which affiliates are increasing traffic to your site, bringing in orders, and driving revenue. Stay up to date on your Amazon settlement report from the Aptos Site Manager. View and manage data from fulfillment centers on incoming and outgoing invoices. Stay up to date on the success of your marketing efforts and membership programs and check on the success of your promotional campaigns. Use Aptos Channel Reports to stay up to date on the progress of your SMS campaigns, promo codes, affiliate sales, and more.
User reports View the progress of your sales representatives in a detailed or at-a-glance format with the commission report. The credit/refund report gives you a breakdown of credits and refunds. You can create a PCI audit use report and an upsell/cross-sell report. You can schedule reports to send reports by email. Keep track of your Site Manager users through the usage report, which shows settings and price changes users have made in the Site Manager, and is an audit trail of their page visits.
Enhanced reports If enabled, gives you the ability to create detailed, ad hoc dynamic reports. For example, the California eWaste Report is located here.
