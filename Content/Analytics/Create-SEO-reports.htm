Creating SEOsearch engine optimization reports

Use this report to see the search terms your visitors are using to find your products from external search engines. Search terms are listed in order, based on the number of clicks produced by each term. This will help you review business statistics for items that are searched for before landing on your site—for instance, to identify highly desired products or brands. Use this information to drive promotions, improve site SEOsearch engine optimization, and adjust inventory for customer demand.

A 3D pie chart displays a percentage and number breakdown for each keyword. A list also appears, showing a breakdown of total searches, total sales, total orders, average order value, and the percentage of conversion.

In addition to the pie chart, three other charts help you compare other metrics associated with keyword searches:

Average Order Value area

The Average Order Value (AOV) for each keyword is measured from the numbers on the left, and displayed as a 3-dimensional column. The number of order items generated by the keyword is measured by the numbers on the right and displayed as a horizontal line across the graph. You can display either the average order value or the number of order items by clicking on the icons at the bottom of the chart.

 Total Sales area

The total sales revenue for each keyword is measured by the numbers on the left and displayed as a 3-dimensional column. The number of orders generated from each keyword will be measured by the numbers on the right and displayed as a horizontal line across the graph. You can display either the total sales revenue or the number of orders by clicking on the icons at the bottom of the chart.

 Total Searches area

The total number of searches for each keyword is measured by the numbers on the left and displayed as a 3-dimensional column. The conversion percentage for each keyword will be measured by the numbers on the right and displayed as a horizontal line across the graph. You can display either the number of reviews or number of Approved reviews by clicking on the icons at the bottom of the chart.

To create an SEO report

  1. In Analytics, select Traffic Reports> SEO Report.
  3. From the Search Filter list, choose the filter you want to use for the report:
    1. Most Searched For – displays the keywords with the most searches.
    2. Highest Total Sales – displays the keywords that generated the highest total sales.

    3. Highest Average order Value – displays the keywords that produced the highest average order value.

    4. Best Conversion Rate – displays the keywords that resulted in a high number of sales when searched.

    5. Worst Conversion Rate – displays the keywords that resulted in a low number of sales when searched.

  4. Click Search.
