Creating Payment Plan reports

Review and manage the status of your customer's payment plans.

To create a Payment Plan report

  1. In Analytics, select Order Reports > Payment Plan.
  2. Use the calendars to choose the Date From and Date Thru and click Search.
  3. Click the pencil icon at the right of the orders notes to view details of the payment plan:
    • ID number
    • To be processed on date. Use the calendar and clock icons to change.
    • Requested On date
    • Job Type, for example, Payment
    • Completed On date
    • Payment Amount, can be edited
    • Options
  1. Click Reset Mark as Complete to mark the Payment Plan as complete.
