Tracking sent orders

Use the Tracking (Sent) screen to view the orders you have marked as shipped, including their tracking numbers.

To display and update an order's tracking information

  1. In Commerce, select Shipping > Tracking (Sent).
  3. Use the Tracking Status list to select whether to track Verified, Unverified, or All Items.
  4. To narrow the search, you can enter the first few characters of the Tracking # if you know it.
  5. Click Search.
  6. To see shipper's information, click the order's Click Here link.
  7. You can update the Tracking # if needed.
  8. You can mark orders as Verified as necessary.
  10. Click Save to retain any changes you made.
