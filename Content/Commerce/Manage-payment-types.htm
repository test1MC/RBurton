Managing payment types

EOM gives you the ability to accept many forms of payment giving your customers complete flexibility with their purchasing options. The more payment options you offer to your customers, the fewer obstacles they have to checking out.

Use this section to view and manage the payment types in use on your site. You can rename the payment methods you have activated for ease of use.

To manage payment types

  1. Go to Commerce > Settings > Payment Types.
  2. To change a name, simply highlight and rename.
  3. Click the Save button at the bottom of the page.
