Managing Carriers

Use this section to add, edit, and delete your shipping carriers, and enter the URL for customer tracking purposes.

To add a shipping carrier
  1. In Commerce, select Shipping > Carriers.

  2. In the blank fields, lists, and check boxes provided at the top of the screen:

    1. enter the shipper Name,

    2. enter their URL (Web site address),

      The URL is displayed on tracking emails sent to customers. You may wish to add a specific tracking URL provided by the company with which you ship.
    3. enter their Phone number,
    4. select the System Ship Carrier— Select Custom Shipping if carrier not in list,
    5. select whether they support QuickShip,
    6. enter a Tracking Prefix for this carrier,
    7. select whether this carrier Handles Returns,
    8. select the carrier's Default Return Box.
  3. Select the appropriate System Ship Carrier from the list provided.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the screen if necessary, and click Save.
 To edit a shipping carrier
  1. In Commerce, select Shipping > Carriers.

  2. Find the carrier whose information you want to edit from the list of those displayed.

  3. Overwrite or replace information for that carrier as needed.

  4. Scroll to the bottom of the screen if necessary, and click Save.
