Managing currencies

Your ecommerce site can be accessed anywhere in the world. If you accept international orders, offers the flexibility to define which methods of currency you accept. It also calculates the currency exchange at checkout.

To limit/expand the currencies you wish to accept

  1. In Commerce, select International > Currency.
    1. To deactivate all currencies, click the Select All link.
    2. To activate all currencies, click the Remove All link.
    3. To select specific currencies to deactivate, select the check box next to the currencies you wish to exclude.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the window, and click Save.

Note: If you choose to deactivate all currencies, remember to enable US Dollar, or customers will not be able to check out.
