Managing subscriptions

Subscriptions are a great way to encourage customer loyalty by making it easy to re-order products at a specified interval. Credit Cards, Cash/Check/Money Order, and PayPal are the only valid payment methods for subscriptions.

Note: ​If you do not have subscription functionality enabled on your site and want to implement it, submit a support ticket or contact your Client Manager. This displays automatically if you have PayPal enabled as a payment option; then set up a subscription product.

The customer's Manage my Subscriptions page can handle multiple subscriptions, and customers can modify their own subscription parameters here. They can update the next ship date, the current schedule and the promo code for each subscription. Customers can also have various subscriptions shipping to different addresses, and different subscriptions can be paid for with different credit cards.

Customers can select the delivery frequency and enter a subscription name for each product on the product page and enter or modify subscriptions on the cart page. If a customer places an order with items having different frequencies or subscription names, then one order is created and multiple subscriptions are created and placed with their own IDs into the schedule table. One shopping cart can hold products with different frequencies and subscription names.

Auto notification emails are included in subscription management. If changes occur in the recurring order: pricing changes, or product availability, can generate an email Email masters can be set up for subscription management at Content > Pages > Emails > Subscriptions. and automatically send to the customer.

​In addition, you can add any type of information or disclaimer You can edit the text that appears here at Content> Pages > Masters > Subscriptions > Subscription Disclaimer. to the bottom of this screen.

Setting up subscriptions

Before you get started managing subscriptions, you first need to set up subscriptions on the Product Details page at Content > Products > Add/Edit.

To define subscription settings

There are several website settings that control how subscriptions will work on your site that need to be configured. Set these up at Users > Website Settings > Payments > Subscription Settings.

About subscription emails

If subscription notifications are identified in Users > Website Settings, an email can be sent to your customers for all found subscriptions notifying them of their upcoming subscription. Edit the Upcoming Subscription Notification email master at Content > Pages > Emails > Subscriptions.

To add or edit subscriptions

  1. Click the CHANGE link under the product name in the Product column.

    A pop-up window filtered for this product opens.

  2. To add products to a subscription:

    1. Search for and select the product(s) you wish to add to the subscription.

    2. Select Add to Subscription, any product you select from the list is added to this subscription.
  3. To replace products in a subscription:
    1. Ensure Add to Subscription is not selected.
    2. Search for and select products you wish to replace in the subscription.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the window, and click (>) Continue to save your changes.
