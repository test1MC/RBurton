Tracking orders (pending)

For customers who are shipping and are not using the Aptos Site Manager’s QuickShip tool, you can enter tracking numbers for all orders that do not yet have tracking details on the Tracking (pending) screen.

When the page loads, all pending orders are displayed. After you click Process, an email is sent to the customer (if relevant), and the status of the order moves to Shipped.

To update tracking and process a pending order

  1. In Commerce, select Shipping > Tracking (pending).
  2. Select E-Mail Options as needed:
    1. Send Review Request—requires Order Reviews to be setup.
    2. Email Customer Copy—sends item update e-mail to customer with tracking information.
    3. Send Me a Copy—sends a copy of the email the customer receives to the person logged into Site Manager.

  3. Update the Tracking # for those orders requiring it.

  4. You can also see ship order and order details for individual orders:
    1. Click an order number to go to the Order Details page.
    2. Click an order's Detail icon to go to the Ship Order page.
  5. Click the Process icon at the bottom of the to process all orders for which you have entered tracking details.
