Using Pick Pack & Ship

Use Pick, Pack & Shipformerly QuickShip to create shipping labels and corresponding tracking numbers, as well as mark orders as shipped for multiple orders all at one time. The Pick, Pack & Ship application works with EOM's label printing application, and offers an integrated label printing and batch processing all in one place.

Prerequisites

In order for labels to be processed and printed by the Pick, Pack & Ship Client application, these conditions or prerequisites must be met.

  • Ensure that the "Default Generate Labels" checkbox is marked in the Express Shipping Rates, Tracking and label Processing panel on Commerce > Shipping Settings . Verify that your shipping carrier credentials have been approved for shipping label generation. If you are not sure, please check with your Client Manager.
  • Verify that the correct order and item statuses are configured for Pick, Pack & Ship. If you are unsure, please check with your Client Manager.
