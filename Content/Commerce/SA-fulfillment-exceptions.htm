Fulfillment exceptions scenarios

This topic covers possible exceptions to basic Store Assocaite order fulfillment procedures and processes covered.

Partial pickup with Aptos POS

If a customer comes in to pick up an order, but declines certain items in it, this requires further actions on the part of the Store Associate:

  1. Go to the Order Worklist, find the order number for the order in question, and click it. You’re brought the order update screen.

  2. Use the minus button (–) to decrease the scanned quantity until it reflects what the customer actually picked up.

  3. Click the Can’t Fulfill Remaining button, and select an appropriate reason.

  4. Click the Ready for Pickup button again to save the changes.

  5. Complete the order at the POS terminal.
