Managing states / provinces

Limit and expand the states and territories to which you may ship.

To manage states and territories to which you ship

Note: The check box deactivates the selected option.

  1. In Commerce, select International > States / Provinces.
  2. Select the country whose states and territories you want to manage from the list.

  3. Select the check boxes for the US States and Territories to which you do not ship.

    You can also click Select All to deactivate or Remove All to activate all states and territories.

  4. Select whether to Deactivate Local Pick Up for each state or territory.

    You can also click Select All Local to deactivate or Remove all Local to activate local pick up for all states and territories.

  5. Enter a Fee Name By State for any that apply.
  6. Scroll to the bottom of the window, and click Save.
