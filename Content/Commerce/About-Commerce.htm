About Commerce

In the Commerce tab you can manage orders, shipping, shopping cart, inventory, and transaction settings. You can view and act upon orders, manage your inventory, and set your shipping rules.

The Commerce menu

Affiliates

Use to set up affiliates and affiliate groups.

 Shipping

Use to add, edit, and delete shipping carriers and enter the URL for customer tracking purposes. The Aptos Quick Ship (QS) functionality allows you to create shipping labels and corresponding tracking numbers, as well as mark orders as shipped for numerous orders at a single time. You can define custom freight settings at the product level. also provides you flexibility in how you process shipments and labels, ship products, and charge your customers for shipping. You can set the harmonization (HS) codes used by shipping companies to identify the categories of products being shipped. You can also create shipping manifests and track sent and pending items.

 Channels

This is where you can create and update the settings for your marketing channels and comparison shopping engines such as Amazon. You will also use this area to set up affiliates.

 Orders

Presents a real-time one-screen view of activities during the order cycle, from the status of the order in general, to specifics regarding the hour, minute and second the order was placed.

You can create orders here, keeping all of your order details in a single location and perform most business related functions within a single interface. Credit card and gift card transactions can be viewed and processed in the orders section. You can also view and manage subscriptions.

 Suppliers

Use to associate products to their suppliers at the product level. The Inventory Report works in conjunction with Aptos ’s Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. ERP is a business management system that integrates all facets of the business, including planning, manufacturing, sales, and marketing. Create and manage POs allow you to easily maintain and submit purchase orders.

 Promotions

Use to create and manage promotion codes and campaigns, add, edit, review or discontinue current site promotions. Offering promotions, coupons, and/or discount codes is a great way to drive business.

 International

Use calculate monetary exchange rates and expand the states and territories to which you choose to ship and bill.

 Settings

Use to manage your chosen payment methods. With FraudLogic, you can configure fraud settings during the checkout process to allow for an automated reviewing process. You have the ability to accept multiple forms of payment giving your customers complete satisfaction and flexibility with their purchasing options.
