Importing and exporting questions and answers

Customers can submit product questions which you can then review, respond to, and publish to a product page to give future customers a better idea about details of a product.

When updating by spreadsheet, you should always export first to ensure you are using the most recent spreadsheet version. This will also guide you on the proper format for each column and row as well as which fields are required.
To export questions and answers
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Questions & Answers.

  2. Use the search filters to list the products from which you want to export Q&A. You can search by any of:

    1. Sub Category,
    2. Brand,
    3. Status,
    4. Product,
    5. review status.

  3. Click Search.

    A list of products that meet your search parameters is generated.

  4. Select the products you want to include in the export.

  5. If you need to, click the black Import/Export Consumer Q&A bar to expand that area of the screen.

  6. Click the Export to Excel button .

    This generates an Excel file and a link to it that appears in the Available Exports to Download area of the page.

  7. Click the spreadsheet link, add or update Q&A information in it, and save the spreadsheet to a local drive. Possible values and meanings for spreadsheet columns are:

    Q&A spreadsheet fields
    Spreadsheet column Description
    Question_ID

    The question ID number used to associate a question and answer set with a particular product. Question IDs are automatically generated by the site manager as a way of identifying a specific question in the database.
    product_id

    The product ID number used to associate a video with a particular product. Product IDs are automatically generated by the site manager as a way of identifying a specific product in the database. If you are unsure of a product's ID, you can find it on the product listing page of Content > Products > Add/Edit. It is displayed for each product under the product title.
    User_Name

    The name of the customer posing the product question.
    User_Email

    The customer's email address.
    User_Phone

    The customer's phone number.
    Question

    The posed question.
    Answer

    The answer response.
    Active

    An active question and answer will be displayed on the product page.
    datecreated

    The date the question was recorded in the system.
    Product_ID1

    Used only if a product has an alternate ID associated with it.
    Product_Code

    The product code.
To import questions and answers
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Questions & Answers.
  2. If you need to, click the black Import/Export Consumer Q&A bar to expand that area of the screen.
  3. Click Browse, find the Excel spreadsheet you want to import, and click Open.

  4. Click the Import button .

    Questions and answers are automatically uploaded to the server, and assigned to the product IDs listed in the spreadsheet. Each new question will be assigned a question ID used to identify it in the database.
