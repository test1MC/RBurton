Adding and editing brands

Set up and manage your brands and brand meta content in the Add/Edit screen.

You can also use the Brands spreadsheet to manage brand information. Find spreadsheet at Content > Import/Export > Brands.

To add or edit a brand

  1. In Content, select Brands > Add/Edit.

  2. Enter the brand or manufacturer's name in the Add Brand field.

  3. Click Add (+).

    The brand is added to the list. Brands are listed alphabetically, so you may need to scroll to find it.

  4. Determine whether this is a Featured Brand; select Yes if so.

  5. To number the order in which you would like the brands to appear on your site, enter a Display Order value.

    You can number all brands or choose only a few. For example if you have 100 brands, you might number only 10 of them (1 through 10), but leave the other 90 brands set to "0" for the display order. Your site will display the 10 numbered items. Depending upon the wiring of your site, this feature may require additional scoping and wiring. Contact your Client Manager for more information.
  6. Click More Details next to the brand name to expand the panel, and enter information in the four tabs there. See the More Details tables for descriptions.

  7. To edit the questions from this list that you want to use for this brand, click the Q&A; button . Save any changes.

  8. Click Save.

Meta Keywords

Do not display on your page. Include between 20 and 30 carefully selected words and phrases; each keyword or phrase must be separated by commas. Make sure you include all relevant keywords and phrases like important product names, high level descriptive terms, materials, manufacturers, brands, geography… even competition keywords of comparable sub-categories.

Because of the importance of meta content, Aptos offers the creation of meta content as a strategic service. If you would like to explore this as a service, please contact your Client Manager.
Meta Description

Describes the content within the Brand, giving search engines' spiders an accurate summary filled with multiple keywords. The Meta description is especially important because it's the only tag supported by some engines. You may wish to use marketing copy in the meta description field for optimal phrasing.

Meta tags are hidden in a document's source and are invisible to the reader. Some search engines, however, are able to incorporate the content of meta tags into their algorithms. No engines penalize sites that use meta tags properly, so it's recommended that you always include them.

Aptos recommends that you upload brand logos. This ensures that your Brand page (if you have one on your site) will be effective, robust, and resourceful.

Exporting brands to a spreadsheet

When you export the brands spreadsheet from Content > Import/Export > Brands, a new display order field appears as the last column in the spreadsheet which you can then be re-import. If this field in empty in the spreadsheet, it will be ignored.

A brand column has also been added to the Excel file, so that you can associate a brand to your products. Go to Commerce > Orders > Export. Select a date rate and click to Export. After the Excel export has been generated, click on it to download.
