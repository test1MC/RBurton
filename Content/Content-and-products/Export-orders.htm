Exporting orders

You can export all of your customer orders created between a specified date range to a spreadsheet.

Click on the Export button at the bottom of the page to export the file. The Excel file will be created and a link will appear in the Report column. Click on the link to open the Excel file.

To export orders

  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Orders.

  3. Click the Export to Excel button .

    This creates an Excel spreadsheet and a link to it beneath the Import/Export buttons.

  4. Click the spreadsheet link, add or update category information in it, and save the spreadsheet to a local drive.
