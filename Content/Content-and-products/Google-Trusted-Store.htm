Setting up a Google Trusted Store job

Before you schedule a Google Trusted Store job, you must have a Google Trusted Store account. To learn how to acquire one, read Google Trusted Stores knowledge base article #2553.

To create a job from a template

  1. Click the Create Job from Template button located at the bottom of the page. A pop-up window opens.
  2. From the form located in the pop-up window, select Generic Google Trusted Store and then Save. This creates an inactive job based on this template.
  3. Close the pop-up window and edit the newly created job.
  4. Create two copies of the Generic Google Trusted Store job, one for shipped orders and another for canceled orders, as shown below.

    1. For Shipping Feed: Set the <CustomField1></CustomField1> field to SHIPORDERS.

    2. For Cancellation Feed: Set the <CustomField1></CustomField1> field to CANCELORDERS.

    3. Set the <CustomField3> to the corresponding Google Trusted Store feed name that you created.

    4. Link your FTP to the steps.

    5. Make certain that each job has a second step for the Google FTP and that the File Name and File Type are named the same as your shipping and cancellation feeds.

    6. The Archive Path must point to the site's \Tasks\Archive\<YOUR SITE>\Google Trusted Store\outgoing\ folder.

    7. The Local Path mustpoint to the site's \Tasks<YOUR SITE>\Google Trusted Store\ folder.

  5. Set the job and the job steps to Active.

Refer to the Google Trusted Stores Guide to learn how to display the Google Trusted Store Badge on your site.
