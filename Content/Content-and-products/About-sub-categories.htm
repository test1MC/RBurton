About sub-categories

In a brick and mortar store, if categories are the aisle headers (for example Frozen Foods), sub-categories help the customer locate products they want in a quick and an efficient manner by defining from a slightly lower level what they will find in the aisle (Frozen Dinners, Ice Cream, and so on).

Set up sub-categories in their related categories to help your customers locate products they want. You can also assign featured content, define and assign comparable specifications, create cross-sell/up-sell groups, and define product order within each sub-category.

Use the Sub-Categories section to manage:

  • Add/Edit Sub-Categories
  • Comparable Specs
  • Cross-Sell/Up-Sell
  • Product Relevance
