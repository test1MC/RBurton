Importing and exporting videos

You can upload videos at the product level via an excel spreadsheet.

You can use this feature only if your site is wired and designed to support videos at the product level. Also, if your site is wired only to accommodate videos stored on the server or uploaded videos, additional wiring changes are needed to incorporate YouTube videos. If you have questions about uploading videos or possible wiring changes, contact your Client Manager.

When updating by spreadsheet, you should always export first to ensure you are using the most recent spreadsheet version. This will also guide you on the proper format for each column and row as well as which fields are required.
To export videos
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Videos.
  2. Click the Export to Excel button . This creates an Excel spreadsheet containing video information, and a link to it in the Report area below.

  3. Click the spreadsheet link, add or update category information in it, and save the spreadsheet to a local drive. Information contained in the spreadsheet includes:

    Video spreadsheet fields
    Field Description
    product_id The product ID number used to associate a video with a particular product. Product IDs are automatically generated by the site manager as a way of identifying a specific product in the database. If you are unsure of a product's ID, you can find it on the product listing page of Content > Products > Add/Edit. It appears for each product under the product title.
    DisplayName The name displayed to your customers as the video title.
    PartNumber Populated if a product has a part number associated with it.
    Video_Name The actual file name for the video.
    Video_Caption A brief description of the video.
    Display_Order You can choose the order in which the videos will be displayed. Optional.
    Delete True or False. Setting to True deletes a video from the database.
To import videos
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Videos.
  2. Click Browse, find the Excel spreadsheet you want to import, and click Open.
  3. Click the Import to Excel button .

    Video information is automatically uploaded to the server.
