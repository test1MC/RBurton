Importing and exporting tier group rules

Use Tier Groups to provide credits based on the quantity purchased and assign fees based on the dollar amount spent. You can dynamically assign fees and discounts to Tier Groups based on the number of items being purchased and the total amount being spent. For more information on managing tier groups and their rules, see Adding and editing tier groups.

You can import and export Tier Groups, or Tier Subcat/Brand Discounts.

When updating by spreadsheet, you should always export first to ensure you are using the most recent spreadsheet version. This will also guide you on the proper format for each column and row as well as which fields are required.
To export tier group rules
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Tier Group Rules.
  2. Below the list of current rules, if needed, click either the Import/Export Tier Groups or Import/Export forTier Subcat/Brand Discount banner to expand that area of the screen, depending on what you want to export.
  3. Click the Export to Excel button . This creates an Excel spreadsheet containing all tier group rules, and a link to it beneath the Import / Export buttons.
  4. Click the spreadsheet link, add or update rule information in it, and save the spreadsheet to a local drive.
 To import tier group rules
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Tier Group Rules.
  2. Below the list of current rules, If needed, click either the Import/Export Tier Groups or Import/Export forTier Subcat/Brand Discount banner to expand that area of the screen, depending on what you want to import.
  3. Click Browse, find the Excel spreadsheet you want to import, and click Open.
  4. Click the Import to Excel button .

    Tier group rules are automatically uploaded to the server.
