Importing and exporting product review articles

Product review articles are exportable by spreadsheet in the Site Manager. This feature provides an easy way to export all product review articles that are linked to all products on your site. If you want to add multiple article reviews to your site quickly, import the spreadsheet.

When updating by spreadsheet, you should always export first to ensure you are using the most recent spreadsheet version. This will also guide you on the proper format for each column and row as well as which fields are required.

Articles imported from a spreadsheet associated with specific products can be seen in the Product Reviews/Articles panel under Existing Reviews, as shown below. This is accessible via Content > Products > Add/Edit within product Details > Attributes.

You can also add information about articles and reviews one at a time to individual products within this panel.

Sites require additional wiring to accommodate product review articles. If you would like to enable this functionality, contact your Client Manager. Please keep in mind that product article reviews are not the same as customer reviews which can be controlled and monitored at Content > Customers > Reviews.
To export product review articles
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Product Review Articles.
  2. Click the Export to Excel button . This creates an Excel spreadsheet containing all articles you have added to your site about your company and various products.

  3. To download the exported spreadsheet, click the Please click here link in the green banner that appears at the top of the window. You can open, edit, and or save the spreadsheet to your hard drive. Product review article spreadsheet fields are:

    Product review article spreadsheet fields
    Field Description
    Review ID The ID of the current review to be updated. Leave blank to insert a new review.
    Product ID The ID of the product to associate to the article being imported. To associate an existing article to a different product, the product ID can be changed to a different product. Required
    Sub-Category The current sub-category of the product for the article. This field can be left blank and is for reference purposes only.
    Product Name The product name of the product for the article. This field can be left blank and is for reference purposes only.
    Review URL

    The URL where the article can be located. Can be a file name or a full http web address.
    Author The name of the article's author.
    Publication

    The publication of the article being imported.
    Review Start Date The start date for the article. If the field is left blank, inserts the current date of the import.
    Review End Date The end date for the article. If the field is left blank, inserts the current date of the import.
    Description Optional brief description of the article being imported.
    Additional Info Any other applicable information about the article being imported.
    Tags Delimited list of keywords to associate to the article for internal site search only.
    Review Type If product reviews are going to be grouped together, this will be a specific type for the current article.
    Delete Article True/False. If the Review ID field is populated and this field is set to true, the current article will be deleted.
To import product review articles
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Product Review Articles.
  2. If needed, click the Import Product Review Articles banner to expand that area of the screen.

  3. Click Browse, find the Excel spreadsheet you want to import, and click Open.

  4. Click the Import button .

    Product reviews are automatically uploaded to the server.
