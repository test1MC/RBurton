Editing automated email masters and content

There are several instances in Aptos EOM and the commerce process in which automated emails are sent to your customers.

About email masters

The content of many of these emails do not require any customization. However, you can edit the content within these standard emails to create more customized messaging.

Important! If your site is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS), when you enter or change the "send from" email address for site emails such as order confirmations, you will receive a verification email from AWS. Follow the instructions from AWS to complete the setup to ensure your emails will work properly.

For each standard email, you can customize :

  • Subject: Update the subject line for the email
  • From: Update the from field
  • cc: Update the cc field (use semi-colons ; to separate email addresses)
  • bcc: Update the bcc field (use a semi-colons to separate email addresses)
  • Third Party List ID: Leave this field blank if Aptos should deliver this email. Alternatively, you may enter a value in this field to indicate that a third party email provider should deliver this email. This value will be passed to the third party email provider. These settings are configured in Users > Website Settings > General > Email Address Settings.
  • There are tokens available for each email. Click on the Description and Tokens to open the email body and view the tokens that you can include in the subject or body of the email. For example, the token [SITENAME] automatically pulls the name of your site if the token is relevant for that email.

About affiliate specific emails

When you create new email templates for affiliates, you can send a test to ensure it is working properly.

To test affiliate specific emails

  1. In Content, select Pages > Emails.
  2. Open any applicable email template.

  3. Click on the Affiliate Specific Email button

    An Affiliate Specific Email pop-up window opens.

    For affiliate specific tokens to be used when sending transactional emails, Overwrite Customer Notification must be selected for each affiliate. Do this by selecting the affiliates at Marketing > Affiliates > Add/Edit. When selected, the [SITENAME] / [AFFILIATENAME] token will pull in the affiliate name when used in the body of the email only. The [SITENAME] / [AFFILIATENAME] token in the subject line of the email overrides the affiliate logic and prefills with your site name.

  4. Edit this template as needed for a specific affiliate.

  5. Click the Email button and send the preview to your, or an admin's, email address.
  6. When received, you can see how the finished email will look to your customers. You can edit and resend it as many times as you need to.
  7. When you are satisfied with your edits, click Save.
