Managing featured products for categories

Manage featured products for different areas on your site. Featured product functionality is available for Categories, Sub-Categories, Brands and certain other pages.

You can also define featured products for 404 Pages, Amazon Checkout, Empty Shopping Cart, and Members Area at Content > Products > Featured.

Note: Featured product functionality must be wired for your site in the Category, Sub-Category, and Brand pages for this feature to work. If your site is not currently wired for featured products on these pages, and you are interested in adding this functionality, please contact your Client Manager.

To add featured products

  1. In Products, select Categories > Featured Products.

  2. Select a Category to which you want to add featured products from the list.

    If there are featured products already associated with the category, they appear on the page.

  3. To customize products at the tier group level, select an appropriate Tier Group from the list. The featured products set up for the category will be added to the tier group you select.

  4. Click the Add button (+) to edit or add featured products for the category.

  5. In the Featured Products pop-up window, add featured products to the category. Use the lists to filter by Sub Category, Brand, product Status, or Name or Part # and click the Search button.

  6. Select the products you want to add to the featured products for the category.

  7. Click Save, and close the window.

    Note: If the products you want to feature belong to different Sub-Categories or Brands, you may need to repeat the above step and repeat the process in the Featured Products window.

  8. From the main page, you can determine the Display Order in which you want the products to appear. You can also delete products by clicking on the Delete button.

  9. When done, click the Save button.
