Managing sub-brands

Some brands you carry may have sub-brands associated with them. For example, the brand Samsonite luggage has a number of sub-brands, including Aramon. Sub-brands are not relevant for many clients. However, if you have brands that are broken down into sub-brands, EOM offers the ability to associate them.

To add and manage sub-brands

  1. Select Brands > Sub-Brands.
  2. When this window opens, enter the Sub-Brand Name.
  3. In the Display Order field, you can determine the order in which the sub-brand will be displayed.
  4. If there is an image associated with the brand, and your site is wired to accommodate displaying the image, click on the Image icon, and a image selection window opens. Browse on your desktop to locate and select the image. Click Save to upload your image.
  5. Use the Associated Brand list to associate the sub-brand with the related brand.
  6. In the Description field enter the description of the sub-brand you want displayed.
  7. Enter the sub-brand Meta Information in the Meta Title, Meta Keywords, and Meta Description fields.
  8. If you want to create additional sub-brands, click the + link to populate new sub-brand fields.
  9. Click Save.
