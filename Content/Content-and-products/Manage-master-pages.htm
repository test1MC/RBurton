Managing master pages

Use page masters to control the messages and images your customers see on your site. This allows you to better manage your content and align it with your overall brand strategy.

Many of these pages require additional website settings for full functionality. To learn more, go to Users > Website Settings > General.

To manage a master page

  1. In Content, select Pages > Masters.
  2. From the list of Masters on the left, click the + toggle to expand the master page categories section containing the master page you want to edit. Click the element/area name to select it.

  3. Make changes as needed, using either the Editor or HTML tab.

    To see a master page's version history, click the See History button at the bottom of the page.
  4. To undo changes made to a page master, click the Undo button located at the bottom of the page.
  5. Click Save.

To view and restore from a master page's version history

  1. In Content, select Pages > Masters.
  2. From the list of Masters on the left, click the + toggle to expand the master page categories section containing the master page whose history you want to see. Click the element/area name to select it.

  3. Click the See History button at the bottom of the page.

    The Master Version History pop-up window opens, displaying the Archive Date, and who archived each version.

  4. To restore the page to a previous version:

    1. Click the Preview button next to the version you want to view. This opens a pop-up window that displays that archived version.

    2. Click the Get button to restore the previous version of this page to the current version. It will be archived with the most recent time stamp and the previous version will still available if needed. Refresh the masters screen to see this version.

Billing Same As Shipping:

Customer checkout can be configured with the billing section expanded (not hidden) at all times and to have the check box function as "My billing address is the same as my shipping address".

At this time, this setting cannot be modified in the Site Manager. If you want to implement this functionality, please contact your Client Manager.

Use this function to change the master text for the check box Billing Same as Shipping in the Payment area. Edit content as desired. To understand how the function works, refer to these explanations:

Checkout for new customers

When checking out as a new customer, in the Payment section they'll see the expanded My billing address is the same as my shipping address text and the billing address. If they want to use the same address they entered in the shipping section, they can select the check box and all information will be transferred. Likewise, if they un-check the box it will clear their entries. If the customer chooses the billing address to be the same as the shipping address, they can still override the copied values.

Checkout for existing customers

When checking out as an existing customer, the functionality will be similar as described above, except the address book entry is displayed rather than individual text boxes for address information. If a customer chooses the billing address to be the same as the shipping address, and then proceeds to select a different address from the address book, the check box will be cleared and the entry that was selected will be used.

Tier Group Credit Card Fee Label:

Tier-Specific Payment Method Rules: Prior to using this master, you must set up Tier Group Pricing settings:

  1. In Content, select Products > Tier Groups.
  2. Click the Add + button to open the Add Tier Group window.
  3. Scroll to Pricing Rules and edit these fields as needed:
    1. Maximum Credit Card Amount Allowed: If the customer is in the Tier Group and his/her cart subtotal exceeds the configured amount, the credit card payment option will not be displayed at the checkout page.
    2. Credit Card Use Fee/Discount: If the customer in the Tier Group chooses Credit Card payment, the associated fee or discount will be applied at the checkout. Use the drop-down menu to choose whether you want this amount to be in dollars or percentage of the order total.
    3. Cash Payment Use Fee/Discount: If the customer in the Tier Group chooses Cash payment, the associated fee or discount will be applied at the checkout. Use the drop-down menu to choose whether you want this amount to be in dollars or percentage of the order total.
Though these functions are useful, your company may want to avoid incurring merchant credit card payment processing fee for large orders. Aptos highly recommends that you only configure these settings if you have other alternative payment options available to your clients.

Would you like to donate?

You can take donations from your customers for various causes at checkout. This is a digital good which appears on the Place Order tab of checkout. Your customers will be able to donate a specific amount or round up their order total to the nearest dollar.

You can set up individual or multiple charities to display at checkout at Content > Products > Add/Edit.

  • The donation title displayed on checkout is configured using the Short Description field, not the product title field.
  • The product title is not what shows on checkout, it is what shows as a line item on the order confirmation and in the site manager.
  • Because this functionality uses this master and the short description field, you can customize your donation section with images, custom text, and so on.

In the Meta Information section, you can manage your meta title, meta keywords, and meta description for the homepage, and the Shop by Brand page of your site. This is also the default meta content for pages within your site for which meta content has not been created.

Ensure names of categories, sub-categories, products and so on, are those which would likely be typed into a search engine by a customer looking for items.

Examples of meta title, keywords, and descriptions are:

  • Sub-Category: Acne Treatments
  • Meta Title: Acne Treatments, Clearing Acne, Acne Cream
  • Meta Keywords: acne, acne treatment, acne product, acne medicine, heal acne, zit, get rid of acne, acne cream
  • Meta Description: Products designed to eliminate acne and reduce outbreaks of unsightly pimples and zits.
Meta ​tags are hidden in a document's source and are invisible to the reader. Some search engines, however, are able to incorporate the content of metatags in their algorithms. No engines penalize sites that use meta tags properly, so it is recommended that you always include them.​
