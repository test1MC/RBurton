Importing and exporting sub-categories

You can edit your existing sub-categories and enter new sub-categories using a spreadsheet to make quick updates. You can also update your sub-category meta content using the spreadsheet.

In general, we recommend that first-time users manually update the Sub-Categories to understand the full procedure. To do so, go to Content > Sub-Categories > Add/Edit, and refer to Adding and editing sub-categories for details.

When updating by spreadsheet, you should always export first to ensure you are using the most recent spreadsheet version. This will also guide you on the proper format for each column and row as well as which fields are required.
Exporting sub-categories
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Sub-Categories.

  2. Click the Export to Excel button .

    This creates an Excel spreadsheet and a link to it at the bottom of the window.

  3. Click the spreadsheet link, add or update category information in it, and save the spreadsheet to a local drive.

 Importing sub-categories
  1. In Content, select Import/Export > Sub-Categories.
  1. Click Browse, find the Excel spreadsheet you want to import, and click Open.
  2. Click the Import button .

    Sub-categories are automatically uploaded to the server.
