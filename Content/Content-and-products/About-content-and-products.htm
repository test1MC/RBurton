About content and products

Use the Content tab to manage site taxonomy; create, edit, and manage products; create blogs; and much more.

Content is one of the most important aspects of your company's website. Good content drives high natural search rankings and ultimately helps drive improved sales. From the Content tab you will create, organize, and manage all of the information on your website. Use this tab to optimize content that customers and search engines see.

Menu item definitions and descriptions

Categories

Categories are the highest level of classification for your products. In a brick and mortar store, think of this as the general overhead sign pointing you to a general department, for example, outerwear, plumbing, frozen foods and so on. Some e-commerce sites with less complex product sets will not need to distinguish by Category. When setting up these e-commerce stores, you can skip straight to Sub-categories and products.

 Sub-Categories

The second tier of classifications (taxonomy) for your products. If Category is the general overhead sign in a store, Sub-Category is the more detailed listing beneath the sign. For example, if Frozen Food is the Category, the Sub-Categories could be: pizza, vegetables, and frozen dinners. Sub-Categories can either always appear expanded, or collapsed and expandable as part of your home page menu.

 Classifications

Classifications further organize your Sub-Category into specific products. Included in this subset are product attributes like: price or material.

 Brands

A Brand contains products across all Categories and Sub-Categories and is grouped by a manufacturer. If all brands are the same, you don’t have to display this in your store. However, when setting up products in , you will still need to associate products with a brand/manufacturer.

 Import/Export

This option allows you to bulk import products without options, or edit existing ones using the Aptos pre-formatted spreadsheet. You should always do an export to ensure you are using the most recent spreadsheet version. This will also guide you on the proper format for each column and row, as well as which field is required.

 Products

Products are the items in your store. You set up your products after you have set up all other Categories. This allows you to associate the individual products with Brands, Categories, Sub-Categories, and so on.

 Blogs

In the Blogs tab you can create, manage, and publish blog content. Blogging is an exceptional way to enhance your SEOsearch engine optimization because you are adding fresh content to your site.

 Locations

If your products are also available in physical stores you can enter store locations in the Locations tab.

 Pages

You can edit existing pages, or create new ones from scratch in the Pages tab. Examples of pages are email templates, click-to-brick, 301 redirects, master pages, and so on.

 Customers

The Customers tab is your communication portal to existing or future consumers.

 International

Aptos has created a tools that define who your international customers are, and translate key phrases on your site for easier accessibility.

 Tasks

Under Tasks you can assign tasks to specific team members such as: to fulfill orders, manage customer requests, and more.
