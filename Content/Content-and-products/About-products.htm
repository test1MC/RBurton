About products

Use the Products section to create and control descriptions, images prices and more. Depending upon the wiring and design of your site, you may can define your products down to the last detail, create personalized options, create and set stock statuses, and define standard featured product areas.

Managing Products in Site Manager is handled through the sections:

  • Add/Edit
  • Copy/Duplicate
  • Featured Options
  • Personalization
  • Stock Status
