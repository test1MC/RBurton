Setting up a Salsify Job

Salsify's cloud-based product content management and syndication solution helps brands and retailers organize and share their content, driving discoverability, sales and satisfaction.

To set up Salsify

  1. Identify the Job Step as "Salsify Product Import".
  2. In the DLL Properties field, enter: ShopVisible.Core.ImportClasses.DLL.
  3. Add a file path to the <CustomField5>. The job will not call the Salsify feed, but instead updates the system using the file on the path.
