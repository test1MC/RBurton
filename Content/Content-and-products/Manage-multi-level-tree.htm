Managing the Multi Level Tree

After your site's Categories and Sub-Categories are set up in the Site Manager, you can see the hierarchy/taxonomy of your company's site down to the product level with the Multi-Level Tree . Use the Multi-Level Tree to assign a product category with a definitive hierarchy. You can also view and assign categories on this page.

This page also indicates which Categories and Sub-Categories are hidden. On the left side (tree), "(hidden)" displays to the right of the Category/Sub-Category. In the available and sort grids, the hidden Category/Sub-Categories use a gray font instead of black.

To add and sort categories
  1. In Content, select Categories > Multi-Level Tree.
  2. Type the name of the new category in the Add Category field and click Add (+).
  3. Drag fields in the Sort Categories column to arrange them as needed.
  4. Click Save.

To manage sub-categories

  1. In Content, select Categories > Multi-Level Tree.

  2. Click on a category in the Available Categories column, and several options appear.

    1. To create and add a new sub-category, enter a Sub-Category Name and Sub-Category Meta Title, and click Add (+).

    2. To add an existing sub-category, select and drag one from the list of Available Subcategories to the Sort Current Sub-Categories.

      Sub-Categories assigned to other top-level categories are not available and do not appear in the list. Remove them from their assigned category first before assigning to another.
    3. To sort sub-categories if your site is wired to do so, drag them up or down in the list of Current Sub-Categories
  3. Click Save.

Viewing categories

Products

You can view where all products fall within a Category and Sub-Category. Expand the Available Categories section to see the number of products in each Sub-Category displayed here. Click on Products within a selected Sub-Category.

This opens the Content > Products > Add/Edit Products window. This window and its functionality is discussed in more detail in Adding and Editing Products.

Classifications

Within any sub-category, click on classifications and the Classifications and Attributes will appear to the right. These options can be expanded to view the Category, Product Type, Parentage and Item Type of the classification within a particular Sub-Category.

Assigning categories

In the Multi-Level Tree, you can view which sub-categories have not been assigned to a category. You can also assign the uncategorized sub-categories with a few simple clicks.

Click the No Category Assigned link to expand the Category and all unassigned Sub-Categories appear. If there are no unassigned Sub-Categories, this option may not appear. You can also assign the uncategorized sub-categories using this function.

  • Add Sub-Category - If you want to add a Sub-Category to the Category, enter the Sub-Category Name of the new Sub-Category and enter the Sub-Category Meta Title. Click (+) Add.
  • Available Sub-Categories - Additionally, you can drag the items in the Available Sub-Categories to Sort the order of the assigned Sub-Categories.
  • Sort Current Sub-Categories - If your site is wired to do so, you can also add Sub-Categories to the Sub-Category list by dragging them from the list of Available Sub-Categories to the Sort Current Sub-Categories column.
Sub-Categories already assigned to other top-level Categories are not available and do not appear in the list. You will need to remove them from their assigned Category first before assigning them to another Category. You will not be able to manipulate a Sub-Category that has a product in it.

It is possible to have as many Sub-Sub-Categories of other Sub-Categories as you choose. However, when creating and sorting Categories, note that products will always reside at the lowest level.

Multi-Level Tree hierarchy example

Here's an example of the possible hierarchy of a (Home) category.

HOME

  • Kitchen (Sub-Category)
    • Coffeemakers (Product)
    • Microwaves (Product)
  • Bathroom (Sub-Category)
    • Towel Racks (Product)
    • Toothbrush Holders (Product)
    • Wall Art (Sub-Sub-Category)
      • Framed Photos (Product)
      • Abstract Art (Product)
  • Bedroom (Sub-Category)
    • Master Bedroom (Sub-Sub-Category)
      • Comforters (Product)
      • Drapes (Product)
    • Guest Bedroom (Sub-Sub-Category)
      • Blankets (Product)
    • Kids’ Bedrooms (Sub-Sub-Category)
      • Bedspreads (Sub-Sub-Sub-Category)
        • Disney Characters (Product)
        • Standard Bedspreads (Product)
      • Curtains (Product)
      • Blankets (Product)
