About the Analytics tab

In the Analytics tab you can view search engine reports, traffic reports, order reports, and more. These reports offer a true understanding of your business.

Menu definitions and descriptions

Here's a list of definitions for the menu items available in the Analytics tab.

Menu item Definition
Order Reports The Aptos platform offers you over 60 customized order reports. You can use this tool to gain insight into specific metrics around traffic, sales, marketing initiatives, and more. You can run reports on items such as: daily trends, payments, repeat purchases, sales trends, top customers, and so on.
Product Reports In this tab you can customize product reports. For example, you can create an inventory velocity report which provides information on the recent sales of each SKU as well as an estimate of how long the existing quantity will last. The most viewed report allows you to analyze your most viewed products. The productivity report allows you to view a list of your most popular items as well as provides a breakdown of order totals and shipping for those items. You can pull reports on products by Sub-Category and use the profit margin report to measure the profit, and not just base revenue, for your sales over a specified time period. Analyze your shipping costs, view top categories, and more.
Traffic Reports Traffic reports can show you by location where your orders are coming from, whether in the US or worldwide. You can also gauge your conversion success rates through our comprehensive cart conversion report. Find out how your customers heard about you and get an at-a-glance view of your search engine rankings for your desired search terms. The source report is a great way to see the search terms that your visitors are using to find your products. You can also get a high level view of affiliate performance, search engine spidering, and what browsers are being used to view your site. Examine the pages most visited on your site, at-a-glance views of the highs and lows of the traffic driven to your storefront, and manage your customer wish lists with the click of a button.
Channel Reports You can monitor which of your affiliates is increasing traffic to your site, bringing in orders, and/or driving revenue. You can stay up to date on your Amazon settlement report straight from the Aptos site manager. You have the ability to view and manage data returned by fulfillment centers to keep incoming and outgoing invoices easily managed to the specific order to which the invoice is associated. You can stay up to date on the success of your marketing efforts and membership programs and check on the status and success of your promotional campaigns. Through Aptos Channel Reports, you can stay up-to-date on the progress of your SMS campaigns, Promo codes, affiliate sales, and more.
User Reports In the user reports you can view the progress of your sales representatives in a detailed or at-a-glance format with the commission report. You can use the credit/refund report to view a breakdown of credits and refunds. Additionally, you can create a PCI audit use report and an upsell/cross-sell report. Through the scheduled reports functionality, you can request basic reports to be sent via email at specific times of the day. Keep track of your site manager users through the usage report. This report will show any settings or price changes that users have made in the site manager, as well as provide an audit trail to their page visits.
Miscellaneous If enabled, this is where you’ll have access to Aptos Enhanced Reporting, where enables you can create detailed, ad hoc dynamic reports that look into underlying database tables. If applicable, the California eWaste Report is located here. You can review the details of your chat history with visitors. This is a great way to evaluate the conversations your Live Chat Representatives have been having with potential customers.
