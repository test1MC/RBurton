About the Commerce tab

Use the EOM site manager Commerce tab you can manage orders, shipping, shopping cart, inventory, and transaction settings. You can view and act upon orders, manage your inventory, and set your shipping rules.

Menu definitions and descriptions

Here's a list of definitions for the menu items available in the Commerce tab.

Menu item Definition
Shipping In the Shipping section, you can add, edit, and delete shipping carriers and enter the URL for customer tracking purposes. The Aptos Quick Ship (QS) functionality allows you to create shipping labels and corresponding tracking numbers, as well as mark orders as shipped for numerous orders at a single time. You can define custom freight settings at the product level. The system also provides you flexibility in how you process shipments and labels, ship products, and charge your customers for shipping. You have the ability to set the harmonization (HS) codes used by shipping companies to identify the categories of products being shipped. You can also create shipping manifests.
Orders In the Orders tab, you are presented with a real-time one-screen view of whatever is going on during the order cycle. From finding out the status of the order in general to specifics regarding the hour, minute and second the order was placed. FraudLogic allows you to configure fraud settings during the checkout process to allow for an automated reviewing process. Credit card, gift cards and eBillme transactions can be viewed and processed in the orders section. You can also view subscriptions, returns and track sent and pending items.
Create Order You can create both retail and wholesale orders using the site manager, keeping all order details in a single location, and perform most business related functions within a single interface. “Best Offer,” which requires an additional build-out, is an exciting feature that gives your buyers and sellers a quick, easy, and safe way to negotiate prices online.
Purchase Orders Use this section to associate Products to their Suppliers at the product level. The Inventory Report works in conjunction with Aptos ’s ERPEnterprise Resource Planning system. ERP is a business management system that integrates all facets of the business, including planning, manufacturing, sales, and marketing. The Drop-Ship Worklist allows a merchant to work across all outstanding orders and to send purchase orders for those orders without needing to access orders one at a time. The PO Worklist provides functionality to easily maintain and submit purchase orders.
Products You can allow customers to pre-order products that are either not in stock, or coming soon. Customers can also get notices automatically when they have asked to let them know when an item is back in stock. This allows you to see which items customers have signed up for.
Promotions From the Promotions tab you can create and manage promotion codes and campaigns, add, edit, review or discontinue current site promotions. Offering promotions, coupons, and/or discount codes is a great way to drive business.
International In the International tab you can calculate monetary exchange rates and expand the states and territories to which you choose to ship and bill.
Company In the Company tab you can manage your chosen payment methods. You have the ability to accept multiple forms of payment giving your customers complete satisfaction and flexibility with their purchasing options.
