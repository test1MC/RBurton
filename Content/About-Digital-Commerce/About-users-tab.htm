About the Users tab

The Aptos Users tab is designed for administrative, user management, and managing your site settings.

Administration functions

In the Administration functions area you can add users and suppliers and manage permissions.

 Website Settings

For security purposes, Website Settings has its own login screen and requires secondary authorization. The Administrator for your site can provide this login information.

The Website Settings section is broken down into five tabbed areas, of which each contains different site-related settings. A brief overview of each of these tabs is provided below. You can expand or collapse panel within in these tabs.

General tab

Use the General tab to change your corporate contact information, adjust your URLs, email addresses, live chat settings and purchase order settings. You can manage these settings:

  • General information
  • URL settings
  • Email settings
  • Aptos server settings (not for client use)
  • PO settings
  • Customer login management
  • Site manager login management
  • Other settings
 Site Layout tab

Use the Site Layout tab to control the layout of your website. These features include general website layout, your shopping cart and checkout processing. Panels in this tab are:

  • Website settings
  • Shopping cart settings
  • Checkout page settings
  • Store fulfillment settings
 Payments tab

Use the Payments tab to add and update payments settings. You can accept many payment types. Here, you can turn on and off all of the various payment types from credit cards through 3rd party payment options. You can also control the subscription processing settings as well.

Each payment method requires a different set-up, and some settings require additional wiring to enable these options on your website. Contact your Client Manager for help with setting up your payment methods. Panels in this tab are:

  • Sales tax settings
  • Credit card processing settings
  • Subscription settings
  • Google settings
  • Paypal settings
  • Amazon payments settings
  • COD settings
  • WU® Pay checkout settings
  • Bill me later checkout settings
  • GE Money checkout settings
  • Wells Fargo checkout settings
  • YBuy Financing checkout settings
  • Cardinal Commerce settings
  • 3rd party gift card settings
  • Alliance Data Services Private Label settings
 Channels tab

Use the Channels tab to manage the main settings for integrated channels. Channel settings control how you interact with Shopping Marketplaces, Social media and other channels. This includes the ability to adjust pre-created tracking pixels such as Cashback and Commission Junction. Panels in this tab are:

  • Amazon settings
  • EBay settings
  • Rakuten settings
  • Shop.com settings
  • Other channel settings
  • Automotive specific settings
  • Social media settings
  • Click to call settings
 Access tab

Use the Access tab to change access features.
