About the CRM tab

Use the EOM site manager CRMcustomer relationship management tab to improve services provided directly to the customers and use the information in the system for targeted marketing and sales purposes. In the CRM tab, you can organize business processes—principally for customer service and technical support. This will enable you to nurture and retain your customers.

Menu definitions and descriptions

Here's a list of definitions for the menu items available in the CRM tab.

Menu item Definition
Communications The Communications tab provides an open line of communication with your customers through integrations like Live Chat. You can read, assign, direct and search for email communications sent and received from your site. If applicable, you also have the ability to integrate with your voicemail technology. If physical catalogs and mailers are relevant to your business, Aptos offers you the ability to manage your catalog requests.
Customers In the Customers tab you can to add, edit and manage customer details. You can also view and manage your internal tickets. You have the ability to import existing customers into the site manager’s CRM. You can also view all of your order notes in one consolidated screen.
Credit Applications If Credit Applications are relevant to your business, and your site is built-out to accommodate them, you use the section to manage your Credit Applications.
Loyalty Program EOM’s loyalty program, which rewards repeat customers, allows them to convert points (based on dollars spent) into either Gift Cards or Promotion codes.
