Navigating in the EOM site manager

The site manager interface is divided into four parts:

Site manager header

The header is split into three sections: company information, site search, and user login information. These are the items found in the header.

Company Logo You can upload your company’s logo to display here.
Search (Knowledge base) Click the search icon, type in keywords and click to search the extensive knowledge base. The search tool will produce a list of results from all knowledge base content.
Search (site manager) The search tool produces a list of results in the site manager. Use at least three characters to search against products, customers, orders, and pages within the site manager.
Set Language Click the page icon to set the default language for the site manager.
User Login Information The user currently logged into the site manager. Individual users can customize the header to display their personalized avatar.
Logout To exit the site manager, click on the avatar and sign out.
Top navigation bar

The EOM site manager navigation tabs and functions are:

Dashboards

The site manager Dashboard provides a quick visual of the live key performance indicators for your company’s website. You can customize your Dashboard settings to get a real-time snapshot of your sales, traffic, current action items, analytics, and more. You can also reach the KPIkey performance indicators Dashboard from here.
Users

Add and set different permission levels for your users and suppliers.

Content

Create, organize and manage all textural and visual information about your business, products and service offerings on your website in this tab. This information is what customers (and search engines) see.

Commerce

Manage orders, shopping cart, inventory, transaction settings, and promotions here. You can view and act upon orders, manage your inventory, set your shipping rules and more.
CRMcustomer relationship management

Manage customer information, communication and ongoing programs with your customers. You can track information in the system for targeted marketing and sales purposes.

Marketing

Manage online channels (Google, Amazon, eBay, Buy.com, and so on). You can set up calendars of events for promotions and sales events as well as create and manage press releases. With the email management system you can create newsletters, thank you emails, promotional offers and more.

Analytics

Your source for complete business intelligence. You can access over 65 standard reports as well as customized search engine reports, traffic reports, order reports, and more. You can also subscribe to Aptos Enhanced Reporting to get an even more detailed look at the health of your company.

Admin

Establish and maintain the users inthe system, maintain the company profile, configure permissions, and more.
Support footer

Gives you access to the Aptos support portal for ticket submission, documentation, downloads, FAQs and more.

Menu navigation bar

On the main page of each tab, you will also see these items:

  • Frequently Used - A navigation pane on the left with your unique most frequently used pages will appear on each tab. As you use the site manager, this section on each tab will populate with your personal most frequently used pages within that tab.
  • Statistics - Unique statistics from your site now appear on the site manager. This enables you to see important statistics at-a-glance. Some even provide clickable links where you can drill in and view more detailed information.
  • Featured Article and Other Recent Articles - Featured Articles within the knowledge base are updated regularly and placed on each tab within the site manager. This gives you quicker access to the most recent articles about the site manager and enables you to reach them more easily.

 Support footer

The support footer located on every page, and contains quick access to the Aptos Support Portal, the knowledge base, various important documents, release notes, and file downloads.
