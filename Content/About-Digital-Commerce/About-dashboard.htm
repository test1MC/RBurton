About the Dashboards tab

The Dashboard provides real-time insight, easy to understand data snapshots, and deep drill-down capabilities into your areas of specific interest or concern. Your executives, managers and staff can compare results with role-based expectations and business objectives. Dashboard reports help you keep track of vital statistics and KPI’s, visualize and track trends on every level of your business and enable you to align activities with key goals. The Dashboard is the default home page of the site manager.

There are four menus available in the Dashboards tab. These menus give you several options to customize your Shop Visible site manager.

Custom

You can customize your own dashboard or view available dashboards to measure your business’ performance.

Configure Your Dashboard

Select this option and a pop-up window opens containing a Widgets tab. While there are many options to choose from to customize your Dashboard, you can use up to 9 of the widgets listed in the Widgets tab.

 KPI

The KPI Dashboard page pulls in data from your website to enable you to see at-a-glance the Key Performance Indicators for your entire site. The statistics on this dashboard draw data from your site in real time. You can also drill into each set of data to see more details and quickly see:

  • Sales Comparison by Year by revenue and quantity
  • Monthly Average Order Value
  • Monthly Sales Quantity
  • Sales by Country
  • Sales by Employee (top 10)
  • Top 7 Products: Inventory
  • Best Customers
 Sales Dashboard

At-a-glance view of annual revenue sales data.

 Traffic Dashboard

Visits by new/returning visitors by source. View demographics and page visits.
