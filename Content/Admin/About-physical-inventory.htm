About physical inventories

Do a physical inventory count to reconcile your system on hand inventory with the actual inventory (units and monetary values) owned by your company. Any variances you discover are the result of procedural or paperwork errors, or theft.

Physical inventory tasks

  • preparing for physical inventory
  • creating inventory control documents (ICDs)
  • importing counts
  • calculating preliminary shrink and adjusting counts
  • posting counts to update InfobaseInfobase, the Aptos transactional database, is a collection of tables that stores the results of merchandising transactions.
  • issuing final shrink reports.

You do most of these tasks in Inventory Management (IM).
