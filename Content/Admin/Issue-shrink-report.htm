Issuing shrink reports

After you post your counts, produce final shrinkage reports that show the values posted to Pending Shrink, Actual Shrink or Beginning Inventory, depending on how your ICD is configured. These reports are your audit trail of what was posted.

You generate reports from the Reports menu. For a final shrink report, use Final as criteria when you run the Query Wizard. For a detailed description of the reports available see Inventory Management Reports.
